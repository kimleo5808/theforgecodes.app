## Detailed Totem Analysis

### Luck Totem

The Luck Totem is the most valuable totem in The Forge and it is not particularly close. A flat +50% luck bonus affects two critical systems simultaneously: rare ore drop rates and [race](/the-forge-race-tier-list) reroll odds. For mining, this means every ore vein you hit has a significantly higher chance of yielding Rare, Epic, Legendary, or Mythical ores. The difference is especially noticeable in late-game regions like Frostspire Expanse and Volcanic Depths, where the base drop rates for top-tier ores are extremely low.

For race rerolls, the Luck Totem increases your odds of landing higher-rarity races when you reroll at the shrine. If you are hunting for a Legendary or Mythical race, activating a Luck Totem before rerolling can save you dozens of attempts. Check our [race tier list](/the-forge-race-tier-list) to know which races are worth targeting.

The Luck Totem stacks multiplicatively with other luck sources. If you are playing a Dwarf or Elf race that already has passive luck bonuses, the totem amplifies those bonuses further. Combine it with a Fortune [rune](/the-forge-runes-guide) for the maximum possible luck stat. This triple stack — race bonus, rune, and totem — is the optimal setup for Mythical ore hunting in the deepest caves.

### Damage Totem

The Damage Totem gives a flat +50% damage increase against all enemies. This is the go-to totem for boss farming and clearing combat-heavy areas like Volcanic Depths. If you are stuck on a boss that keeps killing you, the Damage Totem can push you over the threshold. It also speeds up regular enemy kills, which means faster quest completion and less time spent fighting when you would rather be mining.

The Damage Totem stacks well with the Berserker [rune](/the-forge-runes-guide) and combat-oriented races. If you are running a full combat build, activating this totem before entering a boss arena can cut your kill time nearly in half. Pair it with strong [weapons](/the-forge-weapons-guide) from the Volcanic Depths tier and you will melt through content that previously felt impossible.

### Mining Totem

The Mining Totem provides two bonuses: +50% mining speed and +25% ore yield. The speed bonus means your pickaxe swings faster, letting you clear ore veins in less time. The yield bonus means each vein drops more ore than usual. Combined, these two effects make the Mining Totem the best choice for pure ore farming sessions where you are not concerned about rarity — just volume.

This totem pairs perfectly with the Efficiency [rune](/the-forge-runes-guide) and mining-focused races like Dwarf. If you need to stockpile materials for a big crafting session, activate a Mining Totem and head into the deepest cave you can handle. The speed and yield bonuses compound with your [pickaxe](/the-forge-pickaxes-guide) stats, so a high-tier pickaxe plus Mining Totem is an extremely efficient combination.

Note that the Mining Totem does not affect luck or rare drop rates. If you want rare ores specifically, use the Luck Totem instead. If you want maximum total ore output regardless of rarity, the Mining Totem is your pick.

### XP Totem

The XP Totem doubles all XP gained from every source for 30 minutes. This includes XP from mining, combat kills, quest completion, and any other XP-granting activity. It is the best totem for leveling up quickly, especially during the mid-game grind where levels start requiring significantly more XP.

The optimal strategy with the XP Totem is to save up several completed quests and turn them all in while the totem is active. Quest turn-ins grant large XP chunks, and doubling those chunks can push you through multiple levels in a single session. Combine the XP Totem with a focused combat session in a high-enemy-density area for even more XP per minute.

### Health Totem

The Health Totem grants +3 HP per second regeneration and +100 bonus max HP. This is the most situational totem in the game. For well-geared players, the health bonus is unnecessary because their armor and weapons already handle survivability. However, for undergeared players pushing into harder regions like Frostspire Expanse or Volcanic Depths for the first time, the Health Totem can be the difference between surviving a cave run and getting sent back to spawn.

The regeneration effect is passive and constant — you do not need to do anything special to benefit from it. The bonus max HP stacks on top of your existing health pool, which can help you survive burst damage from bosses and elite [enemies](/the-forge-enemies-guide). If you find yourself dying frequently in a specific area, try the Health Totem before investing in expensive gear upgrades.

## Totem Stacking and Timing Strategy

Understanding how totem stacking works is essential for getting maximum value from your totems. Here are the key rules:

Totems do NOT stack with themselves. You cannot activate two Luck Totems at the same time to get +100% luck. If you try to use a second copy of the same totem while one is already active, it will either be wasted or simply refresh the 30-minute timer. Either way, you lose a totem for no benefit. Never double up on the same totem type.

Different totems DO stack with each other. You can absolutely run a Luck Totem and a Mining Totem simultaneously to get both +50% luck and +50% mining speed plus +25% ore yield. This is a powerful combination for ore farming sessions where you want both volume and rarity. Similarly, Damage Totem plus Health Totem is a strong combo for pushing difficult combat content.

The 30-minute timer runs in real time. This is critical to understand. The timer does not pause when you are in menus, when you are traveling between regions, or even when you leave the game entirely. If you activate a totem and then spend 10 minutes organizing your inventory, you just burned a third of your totem duration doing nothing productive.

The optimal totem strategy is:

1. Plan your session before activating any totems. Decide what you are going to do — mine a specific cave, farm a specific boss, complete specific quests.
2. Prepare everything in advance. Make sure your inventory has space, your gear is equipped, and you are already at or near your target location.
3. Activate your totems right before you start the activity. Do not activate them in town and then spend five minutes running to the cave.
4. Execute your plan without interruption for the full 30 minutes. Stay focused. Every second of totem time you waste is value lost.

For Luck Totem specifically, go straight to the deepest caves in your target region immediately after activation. The deepest areas have the highest concentration of rare ore veins, and you want every minute of that +50% luck working on the best possible veins. Do not waste Luck Totem time mining common ores in shallow caves.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do totems stack with each other?

Yes, different totems stack. You can run Luck Totem + Mining Totem + Damage Totem all at the same time and receive all three buffs simultaneously. However, you cannot stack two of the same totem. Using two Luck Totems will not give you +100% luck — it will just waste one.

### Does the totem timer pause when I leave the game?

No. The 30-minute timer runs in real time from the moment you activate the totem. If you log out, the timer keeps counting down. If you disconnect, the timer keeps counting down. This is why it is important to activate totems only when you are ready to play for a full 30-minute session without interruption.
